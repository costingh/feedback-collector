Struggling to collect great testimonials?

**The form you use matters.**

Here are **copy-paste templates** you can start using today.

---

## Template 1️⃣: Text Testimonial

> Name:  
> Company:  
> What problem did you face?  
> How did we help you?  
> What results have you seen?  
> Would you recommend us?

---

## Template 2️⃣: Video Testimonial

> Introduce yourself  
> What made you choose us?  
> Describe your results  
> Share anything else you'd like to add

---

## Template 3️⃣: Star Rating + Review

> How would you rate us? (1-5)  
> Short written feedback  
> Permission to feature your review?

---

## Template 4️⃣: NPS-Style Feedback

> How likely are you to recommend us? (0-10)  
> What's the main reason for your score?  
> What could we improve?
