**Manually chasing clients for testimonials is exhausting.**

But what if you could **automate testimonial collection** and let happy customers submit feedback on autopilot?

Here’s exactly how to do it.

---

## Use Triggers to Ask Automatically

### 1️⃣ After Purchase

- Stripe, Paddle, Gumroad  
- Trigger testimonial request post-sale

### 2️⃣ After Onboarding

- After client success milestones  
- Completion of setup or onboarding

### 3️⃣ After Positive Feedback

- NPS surveys  
- 5-star reviews  
- Support tickets marked as solved
